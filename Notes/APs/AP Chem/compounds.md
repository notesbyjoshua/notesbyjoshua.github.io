# Unit 2: Compound Structure and Properties

## Types of Bonds

### Ionic
- Metal + nonmetal  
- Electron transfer  

### Covalent
- Nonmetal + nonmetal  
- Electron sharing  

### Metallic
- Sea of electrons  

---

## Lewis Structures

Steps:
1. Count valence electrons  
2. Choose central atom  
3. Fill octets  
4. Add bonds if needed  

---

## VSEPR Geometry

- Linear → 180°  
- Trigonal planar → 120°  
- Tetrahedral → 109.5°  

---

## Hybridization

- sp → linear  
- sp² → trigonal planar  
- sp³ → tetrahedral  

---

## Bond Types

- Sigma (σ) → head-on  
- Pi (π) → side-by-side  

---

## Bond Order

- 1 → single  
- 2 → double  
- 3 → triple
