# Unit 5: Kinetics

## Rate Laws

$$
\text{Rate} = k[A]^m[B]^n
$$

---

## Reaction Order

- 0th → constant rate  
- 1st → linear decay  
- 2nd → curved  

---

## Arrhenius Equation

$$
k = Ae^{-E_a/RT}
$$

---

## Factors Affecting Rate

- Temperature  
- Concentration  
- Surface area  
- Catalyst
