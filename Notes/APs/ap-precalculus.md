# AP Precalculus

**Note**: The AP curriculum is a bit basic, I will add some extra units that are part of precalculus but do not show up on the AP. The units are organized according to the HW Honors Precalculus Curriculum.

---

## Units

Unit pages are not published yet; names follow the [College Board AP Precalculus](https://apstudents.collegeboard.org/courses/ap-precalculus) outline.

- Unit 1 & 2: Fundamentals, Equations, and Inequalities
- Unit 3: Functions
- Unit 4 & 13: Polynomial & Rational Functions and Applications to Optimization
- Unit 5: Exponential & Logarithmic Functions
- Unit 6 & 7: Trigonometric Functions
- Unit 8 & 9: Graphs and Analytics of Trig Functions
- Unit 10: Additional Topics in Trigonometry (Triangle Laws, Parametric, Polar, and Vectors)
- Unit 12: Conic Sections
